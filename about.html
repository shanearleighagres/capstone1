Our Philosophy

Vegetarian cuisine places utmost importance on the quality of ingredients. Its vibrancy, simplicity, and freshness make up most of its appeal, thus these are the principles that we uphold. We are committed to use only the best products we can find. Because we own our own farms and we grow our own produce, we make sure that all ingredients are organic and chemical free. We always preserve the authenticity of the cuisine, and aim to showcase the best of our country with the best of the world.

Our Chefs

Chef Jake

Chef Jake is one of our head-chefs. He graduated at The International School of Culinary Arts and has a passion for modern Filipino and Italian food. He is also a seasonal guest chef at the Food Network Philippines.

Chef Rob

Chef Rob is the second son of Mr. and Mrs Lorenzo. He is also a celebrity chef and is a judge in the Pinoy Masterchef series shown in PTV 8. He also graduated in the International School of Culinary Arts. He is specializing in vegetarian and healthy dishes.
